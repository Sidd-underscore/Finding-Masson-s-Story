Finding Mason's Story

“Finally!” I had thought. “A relaxing ocean vacation!” Oh, I was so wrong. Since you’ve found this, I’m glad I can warn someone not to wander where I did, but, sadly, I can’t send all the records of my disastrous adventures in this tiny Coca Cola can. I do, however, have instructions for you, if you are brave enough to venture in the infernal land I had the misfortune of vacationing on to find out exactly why I am missing. I have split my story into three sagas, to not only complicate your life but to also provide you with first-hand accounts of what is written in what you aim to dig up. Ready to start? Firstly, take a boat to the island found at the coordinates 48.1364° N, 53.6129° W. A pier is located in the South-West corner of the island.

I’m getting tired...
Stand at the end of the pier. The desired object is anywhere within 6 paces of your location. To pinpoint its exact location, you will be glad to know that it is also anywhere within 4 paces of the Bottomless Pit.

The fatigue is setting in...
Stroll up to The Beach. Look down at The Mountains, admire them, but most importantly use your sextant to draw a line that stretches as far as possible 55° to the left of The Mountains using the pails of water by the umbrella. Then, walk down in a straight direction towards The Mountains but stop once you’ve walked five paces. Chapter 2 will be within 4.5 paces from you.

Oh goodness the tiredness is overwhelming...
Hike to the forest. Orient yourself so you’re looking directly at The Tree and use your handy-dandy sextant and measure 40° to the left of The Tree. Then, go to The Tree and orient yourself so you’re staring straight where you just were. Measure 50° to the left of that location. Using that information, you will find the last part of my saga.

Mason Madman
